# Introducing Coolha Business

Coolha Business is a professional dashboard for corporate organizations, community organizations, and creators to conveniently manage account operation data.

Tools and Business:
+ [Coolha Ads](/business/ads.md) Advertise with Coolha
+ [Coolha Shop](/business/shop.md) Sell goods and services in Coolha

## How to use?

Using Business requires the account to have on-chain membership.
